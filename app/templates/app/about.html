Welcome To KrishakGrocery

Krishak Grocery is an online grocery shopping webapp where customers can directly buy fresh fruits and vegetables. It adds the available products of farmers through direct contact and supplies the customer according to their demand. It provides a platform that allows the customers to pay with cash on delivery or via payment gateways like PayPal. With a precise delivery location and address of the customer, deliveries are done with little contact, ultimately decreasing the risks of COVID-19 spread.
